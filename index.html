About Us

Mouthwatering Achar is a proudly South African supplier of premium handcrafted achar, based in the heart of Polokwane. We specialize in large-scale production and distribution of our uniquely bold flavours, catering to a wide range of clients, from national retailers and restaurants to caterers, and wholesalers. We understand that in Mzansi, flavour is everything. That’s why we’ve perfected our three signature options: Hot, Garlic, and Mild, each prepared in bulk with care, consistency, and a serious kick of flavour. Whether your customers are looking for that extra zing with their pap and vleis, or just a bit of mellow spice to top off a sandwich, we've got you covered. Our production process blends age-old recipes with strict hygiene standards, ensuring every litre is packed fresh, full of flavour, and delivered on time. No shortcuts, no drop in quality, just real achar, made for real people. At Mouthwatering Achar, we’re not just about selling products, we’re about building solid, long-term relationships with our clients. Reliable delivery, professional packaging, and an uncompromising focus on flavour, that’s what you get when you work with Mouthwatering Achar. If you’re looking for a supplier you can count on, you’ve just found one. Mouthwatering isn't just a name. It's a promise.
