About Us

Mouthwatering Achar is a proudly South African supplier of premium handcrafted achar, based in the heart of Polokwane. We specialize in large-scale production and distribution of our uniquely bold flavours, catering to a wide range of clients, from national retailers and restaurants to caterers, and wholesalers.

We understand that in Mzansi, flavour is everything. That’s why we’ve perfected our four signature options: Hot, Garlic, Mild, and Mild Garlic, each prepared in bulk with care, consistency, and a serious kick of flavour.

Whether your customers are looking for that extra zing with their pap and vleis, or just a bit of mellow spice to top off a sandwich, we've got you covered.
